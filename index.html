Extending the NFL season from 17 weeks to 26 presents an opportunity to increase overall profitability by monetizing opportunities for digital fan engagement, increase opportunities for athlete recovery, while positioning the league well to further maximize broadcast streaming and licensing agreements. Our forecast predicts both options, 17 weeks and 26 weeks, will result in increased league revenue, however a 26 week format will result in greater overall profitability for the league and individual teams. The longer format notably increases athlete recovery, intended to cause an increase is health, longevity and quality of play. Additionally, an increase in product offerings is an opportunity for media partners to carry live sports coverage an additional nine weeks.
